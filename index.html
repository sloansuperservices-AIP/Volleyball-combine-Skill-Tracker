Vision & Values

Our vision is to create an environment where we provide character development, build self-esteem, and teach valuable skill sets and good sportsmanship. This environment enriches the players, coaches, and parents by incorporating core values such as work ethic, persistence, discipline, determination, competition, goal setting, and accountability.

Character First

We believe that training alongside school programs gives players a rounded development. We want each player to be able to transfer into a college program with the understanding of core skills & the ability to adapt at the next level.

Community

Mid TN VBC was started in 2004 to improve the knowledge and skills of athletes in our surrounding area. We help all players build the confidence & skill set to allow all to reach their potential.

Our Programs

Elite & Regional Teams

Elite Travel (Black): Highest-level competitive teams traveling to USAV national qualifiers and national championships. (6-8 month season).


Regional (Blue/Silver): Competitive teams playing within the SRVA region. (4-6 month season).

Youth Development

Volley Tots (Ages 4-8): Our first step into volleyball, built for beginners. Athletes develop agility, footwork, coordination, and body control through fun, age-appropriate drills that build a strong foundation.


Volley Jrs (Ages 9-13): The next step after Tots. Athletes strengthen passing, setting, serving, and attacking while learning simple game concepts like spacing, communication, and teamwork through fun gameplay.

Training & Lessons

Private Lessons: 1-hour sessions with experienced staff for all levels, from beginners to college-bound athletes. Available up to 2 weeks in advance. Payment is required upon booking. No-shows will be charged the full amount; cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a fee.


Summer Camps:
• All Skills: (6/8-11 or 7/27-30) Learn essentials like passing, hitting, and serving through drills and friendly competition.
• Tryout Tune-Ups: (6/22, 6/29, 7/6) Sharpen skills and build confidence right before tryouts.
• Fun with Fundamentals: (7/20-23) Perfect for beginners; no experience needed!
• Skills Week (6/22-25): Focused themes to level up fast:
  Day 1: Setting + Attacking
  Day 2: Serving + Serve Receive + Defense
  Day 3: Dig-to-Attack
  Day 4: Pass-to-Set


Rec Leagues: Multi-week sessions focused on fun, fitness, and learning with no travel required. Consistent practice times with experienced Mid TN coaches. Offered quarterly.

Training Highlights

Walk-Ups Welcome: All training programs allow walk-up registration. Our large facility at Hooptown can accommodate up to 100 athletes at a time. Registration typically closes 48 hours in advance for staffing, but families are always welcome to register on-site.

Why Play Club?

Club vs. Recreational

A traditional club season is usually 4-6 months, while elite teams with higher expectations may compete in a 6-8 month season. Club requires a sizable investment in time, emotional support, and financial commitment.

Recreational Leagues

In its simplest form, Mid TN recreational leagues are focused on fun, fitness, and learning. Fundamentals are taught to each individual along with team tactics and some competition, with no travel required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I sign in for tryouts?

Check-in is located at the front desk of Hooptown. Please arrive 30 minutes before your scheduled session with all paperwork ready.

What do I need to bring?

Athletes should bring water, knee pads, and appropriate court shoes. You MUST have your SRVA tryout membership confirmation and medical release forms ready.

When will we know about team offers?

Initial offers are typically made within 24-48 hours after the conclusion of tryouts for your age group via email/SportsEngine.
