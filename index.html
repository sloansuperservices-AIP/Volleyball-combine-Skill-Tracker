Our Programs

Elite Travel

Our top-tier national level competition teams. Designed for athletes seeking maximum exposure and the highest level of competition across the country.

Regional Teams

Competitive play within the Southern Region (SRVA). Focuses on skill mastery and strategic team play with a reduced travel schedule compared to Elite.

Youth Programs

Volley Tots (Ages 4-8): Introduction to volleyball fundamentals and coordination in a fun, high-energy environment.

Volley Jrs (Ages 9-13): Skill strengthening and technical development for intermediate young players.

League/Academy: A comprehensive program combining 4 weeks of intensive technical training with 4 weeks of competitive match play.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I sign in for tryouts?

Check-in is located at the front desk of Hooptown. Please arrive 30 minutes before your scheduled session with all paperwork ready.

What do I need to bring?

Athletes should bring water, knee pads, and appropriate court shoes. You MUST have your SRVA tryout membership confirmation and medical release forms ready.

When will we know about team offers?

Initial offers are typically made within 24-48 hours after the conclusion of tryouts for your age group via email/SportsEngine.
