Our Programs

Elite Travel

Our top-tier national level competition teams. Designed for athletes seeking maximum exposure and the highest level of competition across the country.

Regional Teams

Competitive play within the Southern Region (SRVA). Focuses on skill mastery and strategic team play with a reduced travel schedule compared to Elite.

Youth Training League

Specifically for grades 2nd-5th. One weeknight of practice and match play at Hooptown. Perfect for developing fundamentals in a fun, controlled environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I sign in for tryouts?

Check-in is located at the front desk of Hooptown. Please arrive 30 minutes before your scheduled session with all paperwork printed.

What is the cost of tryouts?

Pre-registration is $50. Walk-up registration on the day of tryouts is $60.

What do I need to bring?

Athletes should bring water, knee pads, and appropriate court shoes. You MUST have your SRVA tryout membership and medical release forms ready.

When will team offers be made?

Initial offers are often made within 24-48 hours after the conclusion of tryouts for your age group.
