About Us

Mid TN Volleyball Club was started in 2004 to improve the knowledge and skills of middle and high school age girls in the Murfreesboro area. We focus on character development, self-esteem, and high-level training.

Main Training: Hooptown Sportplex, Smyrna TN

Beach: Sportscom, Murfreesboro TN

Club

Ages 12-18. High-level competitive teams and travel.
