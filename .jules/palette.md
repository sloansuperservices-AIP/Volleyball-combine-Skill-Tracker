## 2026-07-16 - [Micro-UX: Clipboard Feedback]
**Learning:** Immediate visual feedback after a "copy to clipboard" action (e.g., changing button text to "Copied! ✓") significantly reduces user uncertainty and prevents redundant clicks, especially in modal workflows where the primary focus is on quick data handling.
**Action:** Always implement temporary state-based feedback for clipboard operations in data-heavy administrative interfaces.
